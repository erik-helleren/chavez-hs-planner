This tool will automatically generate a summer school status according to this document



To use this tool. You'll need 4 files.

The files can either retain their original names, or you can rename them according to the instructions


  1. Grades Extract from Gradebook
    • CPS Gradebook
      •
    • Click Reports
    • Click ES Cumulative Grades Extract
    • Click Save As .csv
    • If you want to rename this, it should have the word "Grade" in the filename
  2. NWEA CDF from Last Year
    • CPS Dashboard
      •
    • Click on School Reports
    • Under "Student Level", click "NWEA CDF & Participation Report"
    • Select your school, the term and students currently enrolled in your school
    • Click on your District Name
    • Click on View CDF Data
    • Download as CSV
    • If you want to rename this, it should have the word "NWEA" in the filename
  3. NWEA CDF from This Year
    • CPS Dashboard
      •
    • Repeat Previous Steps but choose a term from this year
  4. Diverse Learners Report as CSV
    • CPS Dashboad
      •
    • Click on the arrow the the right of the School Profile Menu option (top)
    • Select IEP
    • Click anywhere in the Grid
    • Filter for your school and all grades
    • Download as CSV
    • If you want to rename this, it should have the word "DL" or "Special" in the filename
